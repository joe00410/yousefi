History

Chelsea Football Club is a professional football club based in London, England. Founded in 1905, Chelsea have been one of the most successful clubs in English football.

The club's home ground is Stamford Bridge, where they have played since their establishment. Chelsea's traditional kit colors are royal blue shirts and shorts with white socks.
